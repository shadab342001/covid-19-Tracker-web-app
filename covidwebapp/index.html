Prevention Coronavirus

Protect yourself from COVID-19 by wearing a mask in public, maintaining a distance of at least 6 feet from others, and washing your hands frequently with soap and water for at least 20 seconds. Avoid touching your face with unwashed hands, and stay home if you feel unwell. Following these simple steps can help keep you and your community safe.

  • Wash Your Hands For 20sec

    Wash your hands for at least 20 seconds. It's one of the most effective ways to prevent COVID-19.

  • Cover Nose and Mouth When Sneezing

    Cover your nose and mouth with a tissue or your elbow when sneezing to prevent the spread of COVID-19.

  • Wear A Mask If Available

    Wear a mask if available to help prevent the spread of COVID-19.
